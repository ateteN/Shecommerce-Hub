Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items. Items must be returned in their original packaging with proof of purchase.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and times vary based on the destination. You can view the rates at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website.
